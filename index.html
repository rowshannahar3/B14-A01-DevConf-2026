Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about DEVCONF 2026.

Is there a student or group discount?

Yes. Students get 40% off Standard passes with a valid .edu email, and teams of 10+ can use the Team tier or contact us directly for custom group pricing.

Can I attend remotely if I can't travel?

DEVCONF 2026 is an in-person event, but every Pro and Team ticket includes lifetime access to full session recordings, so you won't miss any of the technical content.

What's your refund and cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Within 30 days, tickets can be transferred to a colleague at no extra cost — just email us your ticket number

Will I receive a certificate?

Absolutely! Every registered participant will receive a digital certificate after the event.

Where can I ask something that's not covered here?

Reach out any time at hello@devconf2026.dev — our team typically replies within one business day.
